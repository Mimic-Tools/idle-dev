Story

A beam of sunlight reflects off of a dirty window pane and shines right in your eye, stirring you from your slumber. Your head feels heavy, as if rousing from an eternal sleep. Muffled sounds of life - voices, the pats of feet on cobblestones - start to be coherently audible amongst a general hubbub of the town. Rising to your feet, you find yourself in an alleyway with little to your person aside from a set of rags that barely cover your person. You try to remember how you got here, where exactly this was, but come up blank. As you emerge from the dark sidestreet, the town sprawls out before you. Without a penny to your name, you walk out, to at least explore your surroundings...
